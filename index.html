About Us

The Las Vegas Aces® strive to not only serve you, but also serve the community. With every jersey sold this season, we will donate 75% of all sales to the Las Vegas Rescue Mission. Our donations go toward helping the homeless and the hungry in the Las Vgas Community. Help us in aiding those in need of food and a safe place to sleep!


William P. Foley II


Our Owner, William P. Foley II, strives to bring joy and entertainment to the people of Las Vegas. He also has a dedicated interest in serving the community by helping those in need of food, water, and a place to sleep. In 2018, William bought the Las Vegas Golden Knights. In 2019, William also bought the Las Vegas Aces. Now William wants to increase his philantropy efforts by getting the community involved. He has personally pledged to match the donations from the sales of jerseys during the first season. He hopes to the community can come together to raise money and enjoy sports.
